ONE Fight Night 39: Rambolek vs. Dayakaev was a combat sports event produced by ONE Championship that took place on January 24, 2026, at Lumpinee Boxing Stadium in Bangkok, Thailand.

Background

[edit]

A bantamweight Muay Thai bout between Rambolek Chor.Ajalaboon and Abdulla Dayakaev headlined the event.[1]The bout originally scheduled met at ONE Fight Night 37 in November 2025, but the bout was canceled after Dayakaev has a hand infection.[2]

At the weigh-ins, one fighter failed to hydration test and missed weight for their respective fights:[3]

  • João Pedro Dantas weighed in at 160.8 pounds, 5.8 pounds over the featherweight limit and he was fined 30 percent of his purse which went to Mohammad Siasarani.
